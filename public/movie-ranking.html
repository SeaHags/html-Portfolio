The Best Shows for my Toddler...that I enjoy as well

My top 3 favorites:

Peppa Pig

Peppa pig is not only adorable, it is visually pleasing and calming.

The Tiny Chef Show

Both my 2 year old and I LOVE this stop motion animation that follows a little chef that lives in a stump.

Sesame Street

I swear my 2 year old daughter is smarter because of this show. I may also have gained some knowledge as well!
